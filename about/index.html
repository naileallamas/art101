NAILEA LLAMAS

GAME DESIGNER, WRITER, NEW MEDIA ARTIST

Nailea Llamas (he/they) has worked in a wide range of media from video games to short-story writing, digital art and illustration. He received a Bachelor's Degree in Art and Design: Games and Playable Media from the University of California, Santa Cruz.

As a writer, I explore human emotions and connection through narrative storytelling. I want to create spaces in which people can have the time to reflect and exist in their head. To describe feelings that cannot be explained. To have time set aside to experience these emotions.
