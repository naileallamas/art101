Challenges:

A challenge in this lab was to try and figure out how to pseudocode in a way that could be translated into code.

List of High-Level Tasks

  1. Find a recipe

  2. Make sure you have the ingredients

    1. If not- go get ingredients

  3. Prepare your workspace/ preheat ovens/ sanitation

  4. Make the dry and wet mix

  5. Mix the dry and wet mix

  6. Make into balls

  7. Place balls on cookie sheet/ bake balls

    1. Watch balls to make sure they don’t burn

  8. Take cookies out

    1. Let cookies cool after taking them out

  9. Remove cookies from sheet

  10. Eat cookies
