Lab 4 Pseudocoding and Problem-solving

This Lab was to get in the habit of thinking in terms of pseudocoding before jumping into coding.

Challenges

A challenge in this lab was to try and figure out how to think like a computer would when giving it directions on how to play the game we chose.

Results

List of high-level tasks.

  • Buy bread, peanut butter, and jelly from Safeway.

  • Walk home with your ingredients.

  • Get out the plate and knife.

  • Wash your hands.

  • If you have a toaster, toast your bread.

  • Lay out the two slices of bread.

  • Spread the peanut butter on the first slice of bread.

  • Spread the jelly on the second slice of bread.

  • Combine!
